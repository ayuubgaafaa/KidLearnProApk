Privacy Policy

🛡️ KidLearn Pro is designed for children. We take privacy very seriously and do NOT collect personal data from children.

1. Introduction

KidLearn Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is an educational application designed for children aged 2–8 years old. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

By using KidLearn Pro, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect Minimal

KidLearn Pro is designed to work without collecting personal information. Specifically:

  • We do NOT collect names, emails, or phone numbers from children
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT use advertising trackers
  • We do NOT share data with third parties
  • We do NOT have social features or chat

3. Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device (local storage) and never sent to any server:

  • Child's first name (optional, set by parent)
  • Stars and progress scores
  • Parental PIN (stored locally)
  • App settings (sound, speech preferences)
  • Premium unlock code (stored locally)

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

KidLearn Pro is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • All app features work completely offline
  • No account registration is required for children
  • Parents control all settings via PIN-protected dashboard

5. Payment & Premium

Premium subscriptions are processed securely through PayPal. We do not store payment information. PayPal's privacy policy applies to all transactions.

  • Payment is handled entirely by PayPal
  • We only receive confirmation of successful payment
  • No credit card data is stored in our app

6. Third-Party Services

KidLearn Pro uses the following third-party service:

  • PayPal — for Premium subscription payments only

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising SDKs.

7. Parental Controls

KidLearn Pro includes a PIN-protected Parent Dashboard that allows parents to:

  • Set and change the child's name
  • Monitor learning progress and stars
  • Enable or disable subjects
  • Toggle sound and speech settings
  • Reset all progress data
  • Activate or deactivate Premium access

8. Advertising

KidLearn Pro contains no advertisements. We believe children's apps should be free from commercial influence.

9. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on the device, security depends on the device's own security measures. We recommend parents set a device PIN/password for additional protection.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
